Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban by J.K. Rowling
is a thrilling and magical series. In this gripping tale, Harry returns to Hogwarts for his third year and encounters the mysterious prisoner Sirius Black, who has escaped from the heavily-guarded Azkaban.
Packed with dark secrets, unexpected twists, and powerful revelations, the story explores themes of friendship, courage, and identity in a truly captivating way.
